  • 12/3/2024 - New Rule, and Eaglercraft 1.12 news!! - edited 12/4/2024

    Hello! recently I found that a lot of 1 person nations have formed. Now, I'm not super mad, but the whole point of the Nation server type is for people to work together to make a wonderful nation, with incredible builds, and to conquer other nations, but you can't really do that in a 1 person nation.
    That is why I made a new rule, it states that"1 Person nations are not allowed", "If a nation is found to only have 1 member after 4 days of the nation being created, it will be disbanded.", and "If you break this rule 2 times, you will be banned from creating new factions for 60 days."
    Boring rule stuff aside, Eaglercraft 1.12 news time.
    Recently the developers of Eaglercraft 1.12 have announced that they will be making it public later this week, they also mentioned that it's likely there will be quite a bit of bugs at first. So to make us of help, and to test how buggy Eaglercraft 1.12 is, I will be making Eaglercraft 1.12 the default client on this website. Please if you run into any bugs let me know so I can let the developers know about it, so they know what to fix. If the bugs do cause too much of an issue though, don't worry, the server will still be 1.8, and I will still leave the 1.8 Eaglecraft client on the website, accessible by holding the "O" key while clicking the "Play!" button, or you can click the arrow by the play button, and select "EaglercraftX (1.8)"
    Once we find that Eaglercraft 1.12 is stable enough I will change the server to a 1.12 server. Currently how I'm thinking I'll do that, is I will just keep the same world we have, and I'll extend it to introduce the new 1.12 world generation.
    EDIT: sorta related to this, but there's now a EaglercraftX WASM client you can use, using the little arrow by the play button as similarily mentioned in this blog post initially.
    Well that was a lot of stuff, probably the biggest blog post, not sure how to end a blog post, I guess just, hope you're enjoying the server so far!

  • 8/8/2024 - Possible server type change?

    Hello, been awhile since the last blog post, anyway because school will be starting up and people are more likely to actually want to play, I'm going to restart the server world. With that being said I also want to make sure that everyone enjoys the server, which is why I'm holding a poll to have you guys vote on what kind of server you want it to be.
    The current types that you can vote on are...

    A simple, friendly, survival building server.

    • This is what the server started as, where killing, stealing, griefing, etc. are disallowed, there are no faction plugins, and a dynmap is there for everyone to see where anyone is.

    A nation server.
    • This is the type we tried during the summer, where killing, stealing, griefing etc. are only allowed during wars, wars can only start with specific situations, people are encouraged to make a faction with multiple people and stay in one base, people are encouraged to be diplomatic with other nations, and a dynmap is also there. (Just want to say that, I know that this didn't work all too well during this summer, but I imagine during the school year where more people are likely to be more active, it'll be a lot more fun.)

    Or maybe a faction server.
    • We have not done this type whatsoever, this is chaos, killing, stealing, griefing, etc. are allowed, period. People are encouraged to be in a faction with people, and dominate the server. A dynmap would not be there, in order to keep people safe, and their bases hidden.

    You can vote on this by either texting me, telling me in person, telling someone who has contact with me, or (preferably) joining the discord server linked above and voting in the #announcements channel. If you have any questions about this please ask me. The poll ends on August 13th, at 12 PM.
